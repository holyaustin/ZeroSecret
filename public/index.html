ZKP-BASED
ANONYMITY MESSAGE
ZkBlind is a zero-knowledge proof-based anonymity platform designed to enable employees or organization members to share potentially sensitive information while protecting their identity. This allows them to express concerns, observations, and criticisms without fear of retribution or backlash. By ensuring users' privacy and the security of their information, ZkBlind encourages more honest and candid feedback, ultimately leading to a more transparent and effective workplace or organization.
